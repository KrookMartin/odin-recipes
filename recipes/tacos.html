Tacos

Description

Tacos are a versatile and delicious Mexican dish made with a folded tortilla filled with a variety of flavorful ingredients. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or gatherings.

This version includes seasoned ground beef, fresh toppings, and a zesty kick, wrapped in soft or crispy tortillas.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Taco seasoning
  • Small tortillas
  • Lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in a skillet and add taco seasoning.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  3. Fill each tortilla with meat and desired toppings.
  4. Serve immediately with salsa or guacamole.
