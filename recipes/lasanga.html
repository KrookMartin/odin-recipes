Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, tender pasta, and creamy cheese. It's a hearty and satisfying meal perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, and herbs

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce with garlic, onion, and tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  5. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
